# Business Problem

Organisations need employees to acquire new certifications in AI, cloud, data and cybersecurity. However, certification planning is often fragmented.

Common issues:
- learners choose certifications that do not match business needs;
- managers lack visibility into team readiness;
- study plans ignore workload and focus time;
- assessment questions are not grounded in approved knowledge;
- organisations cannot easily connect learning investment to future skills demand.

## Target Users

- Learners
- Managers
- Learning and Development teams
- Certification programme owners
- Workforce planning teams

## Value Proposition

AI Learning Parliament provides balanced certification decisions by combining:
- learning content;
- assessment readiness;
- work capacity;
- business value;
- career development;
- future skills demand;
- critic validation.

## Differentiator

Instead of a single learning assistant, the solution uses a parliament of specialised agents that debate and validate certification recommendations.
