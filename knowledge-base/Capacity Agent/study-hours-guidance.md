# Study Hours Guidance

## AI-102 Study Guidance
Recommended weekly study effort: 6 to 8 hours per week.
Fast-track preparation: 10 or more hours per week.
Minimum viable pace: 4 hours per week.
Below minimum pace: less than 4 hours per week.

Support when study hours are 6 or more per week.
Conditional Support when study hours are 4 to 5 per week.
Oppose when study hours are below 4 per week.
