# Workload Risk Framework

## Workload Levels
Low workload: low risk.
Medium workload: manageable risk.
High workload: meaningful risk to study consistency and delivery focus.
Very High workload: strong risk to learning capacity and operational execution.

## Burnout Risk Signals
Burnout risk increases when workload is high, meeting load is high and study time is low.

## Decision Rules
Support when workload risk is low.
Conditional Support when workload risk is moderate or elevated.
Oppose when workload risk is high and learning is unrealistic.
