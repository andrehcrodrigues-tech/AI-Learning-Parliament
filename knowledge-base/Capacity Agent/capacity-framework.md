# Capacity Framework

## Weekly Study Capacity
High Capacity: 10 or more hours per week.
Moderate Capacity: 6 to 9 hours per week.
Limited Capacity: 4 to 5 hours per week.
Low Capacity: below 4 hours per week.

## Meeting Load
Low: 0 to 10 hours.
Moderate: 11 to 20 hours.
High: 21 to 30 hours.
Very High: above 30 hours.

## Decision Rules
Support when study capacity is moderate or high and workload is manageable.
Conditional Support when capacity is limited but feasible.
Oppose when preparation is unrealistic.
