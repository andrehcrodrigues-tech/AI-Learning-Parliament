# Manager Framework

## Purpose
Supports the Manager-Agent.

## Manager Evaluation Areas
Managers evaluate certification decisions using:
- manager goals
- team goals
- delivery impact
- operational risk
- team capability growth
- short-term execution feasibility

## Decision Rules
Support when certification supports delivery and capability goals with acceptable operational impact.
Conditional Support when alignment exists but workload, timing or delivery risk must be managed.
Oppose when the certification creates unacceptable delivery or operational risk.
